About openFDA & This Survey

OpenFDA was created by Taha Kass-Hout, MD, MS, the FDA's first Chief Health Information Office. The platform launched in beta mode in June, 2014, and provides APIs to several high-value structured datasets, including drug adverse event reports, medical device reports, and enforcement reports.

The Women's Health Adverse Event survey is intended as real-time mechanism for women consumers to report adverse events, and as a tool for physicians to educate patients and raise awareness of adverse events.
